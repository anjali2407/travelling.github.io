The Bargi Dam is one of the first completed dams in a series of 30 major dams to be built on the Narmada River in Madhya Pradesh , India . Narmada is the largest river of Madhya Pradesh, which flows westwards and joins the Arabian Sea . Its total length is 1312 km, out of which 1072 km flows through the land of Madhya Pradesh.
