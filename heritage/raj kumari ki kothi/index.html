Description

Rajakumari Bai Kothi is a majestic Italian style mansion built by Raja Gokuldas for his grand daughter. But, in later years it became a part of a famous Royal Hotel. Now this historic mansion is occupied by Madhya Pradesh Electricity Board. Jabalpur City, 336 km from Bhopal, is well connected by a good network of road and rail.

Location

abalpur, Govindh Bhavan Colony, South Civil Lines, Madhya Pradesh.
